Pet Policy

This addendum permits the guest to bring a pet(s) to the property subject to the following conditions:

  1. Pet(s) must be kept under control at all times and not left unattended inside the property.
  2. Pet(s) are not permitted on furniture or beds.
  3. Guest is responsible for cleaning up after pet(s) on the property and surrounding areas.
  4. Any damage caused by pet(s) will be charged to the guest. Damage charges will be deducted from the security deposit and any excess billed directly.
  5. Guest agrees to have pet(s) flea-treated before arrival.
  6. Excessive pet hair or odor requiring special cleaning will result in additional cleaning fees.
  7. Pet(s) must not disturb neighbors. Excessive barking or noise may result in early termination of the reservation without refund.

By signing below, the guest acknowledges and agrees to all pet policy conditions stated above.
